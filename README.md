# XOXO LEGO® Sets

## Washington High LEGO® Set

<img src="Washington%20High%20Lego%20Set/Washington%20High%20Render%201.jpg" alt="Washington High LEGO® Set" width="600">

The model is based on the layout of XOXO 2024, featuring our venue, Washington High, our entrance structure, outdoor tent, food carts, and picnic tables. The rear of the model opens to reveal a LEGO® microfigure conference speaker standing in front of a custom printed XOXO Explore logo tile.

- [Model File](Washington%20High%20Lego%20Set/Washington%20High%20BrickLink%20Model.io) (Bricklink .io)

## Explore Logo LEGO® Set

<img src="Explore%20Logo%20Lego%20Set/Explore%20Logo%20Render%201.jpg" alt="Explore Logo Lego Set" width="600">

The model features our new “XOXO Explore” logo, designed by Jen and Richard from [Skinny Ships](https://www.skinnyships.com/), and is made of 324 1x1 LEGO® tiles in all eight festival year colors.

- [Build Instructions](Explore%20Logo%20Lego%20Set/Explore%20Logo%20Instructions.pdf) (.pdf)
- [Model File](https://github.com/xoxo/lego/blob/main/Explore%20Logo%20Lego%20Set/Explore%20Logo%20BrickLink%20Model.io) (Bricklink .io)
